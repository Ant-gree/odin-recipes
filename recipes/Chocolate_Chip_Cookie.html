Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

A delicious recipe for chewy, delectable cookies that are sure to impress! Either cook them today or freeze for later.

Ingredients
  • 2 3/4 cups of Flour
  • 2 1/2 sticks of softened butter
  • 1 3/4 cups of packed dark brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup of white sugar
  • 1tsp each of baking powder and baking soda
  • 1.5 tsp of salt
  • 2 room temp eggs
  • 2 tsp of vanilla extract
  • 1 package of chocolate chips
Steps
  1. Sift Flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt into a bowl, set aside
  2. Add butter and sugar into a mixing bowl
  3. Cream together butter and sugar until butter mixture lightens
  4. Add in eggs, one at a time, mixing slowly after each egg to make sure they're incorporated
  5. Add in vanilla extract
  6. Mix in Chocolate Chips or mixings by hand
