Pot Roast

Description

This is a slow cooker recipe, perfect for those lazy Sundays!

Ingredients
  • 3 lb Pot Roast
  • 2 lbs Yellow Potatoes
  • 1.5 lbs Carrot
  • 1.25 cupsBeef Broth
  1. Sear Pot roast over high heat to develop a crust
  2. Add broth, potatoes, carrot, and roast to slow cooker
  3. Cook for 5-6 hours, until vegetables are tender
  4. Serve and Enjoy!
